### What each rank does

**Rank 1: Boy**

- Skill: Revolution
- When you discard it *the **first** time*, its skill won't activate. 

  But when you discard it the **second** time, you get to call on one of the players, make him or her draw a hand and expose both of his or her hands in public.

  AND on top of that, you get to choose one of them for him or her to discard.

**Rank 2: Soldier**

- Skill: Investigation
- You call on one of the players and guess what card he or she has.

  If you guess it right, he or she loses. Else, nothing happens.

**Rank 3: Fortune teller**

- Skill: Clairvoyance
- You get to see one of the players' hand in person, but you are NOT ALLOWED to tell the other players.

**Rank 4: Girl**

- Skill: Protection
- Until your next turn, it makes any attacks you get from the other players ineffective.

  It is possible that the other players still choose to attack you, but no use.

**Rank 5: Grim reaper**

- Skill: Epidemic
- Similar to the second time you discard Rank 1 --- you get to call on one of the players, make him or her draw a hand, BUT you don't get to see his or her hands.

  AND you choose one of them for him or her to discard.

## Rules

### Initial setup

1. Shuffle the deck

2. Each player draws a hand (DO NOT SHOW IT TO YOUR OPPONENTS)

3. Pick a card from the deck with its face down and place it horizontally on the table

   (We call it "reincarnation card")

   **Note:** When you have a Rank 10(hero) and your opponent makes you discard it for some reason other than them using a Rank 9(emperor) against you, you get to resurrect ONCE using that reincarnation card.
